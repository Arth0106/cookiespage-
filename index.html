Cookies

Cookies are small, sweet baked treats that come in a variety of flavors and textures. They can be soft and chewy or crisp and crunchy, often enjoyed as a snack or dessert. Common ingredients include flour, sugar, butter, and eggs, with additions like chocolate chips, nuts, or dried fruits to enhance flavor. Cookies are popular worldwide and can be homemade or store-bought, making them a versatile and beloved treat for all ages.
